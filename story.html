OUR STORY

Born from the Desert, Shaped by Science

Every Chlorice product carries a piece of Béchar — the warmth of the Saharan sun, the resilience of its botanicals, and the curiosity of a young scientist who dared to dream.

WHERE IT ALL BEGAN 2023

A Seed Planted in a University Lab

It started with a question only a biology student would ask: "What if the plants growing in our backyard hold the key to radiant skin?" In the quiet labs of Béchar's university, between microscopes and desert botanicals, the idea for Chlorice was born — not as a business, but as a passion project. A curious mind, a love for science, and a deep connection to the land of the Sahara planted a seed that would soon blossom into something extraordinary.

THE TURNING POINT 2024

From the Lab to the Stage: Blue Hack

What began as a university dream took its first bold step into the world at Blue Hack — a national hackathon dedicated to innovative women in the blue economy. Surrounded by visionaries and mentors, the founder pitched Chlorice's vision: skincare that honors tradition, respects nature, and is backed by real science. The experience was transformative — earning recognition, building confidence, and proving that a girl from Béchar could compete on any stage.

MEETING THE WORLD 2025

Showcased at the Traditional Crafts Exhibition

Chlorice earned its place among Algeria's finest artisanal brands at the Traditional Crafts Exhibition. For the first time, customers could touch, smell, and experience the products — and the response was overwhelming. It was more than a booth; it was a conversation between heritage and innovation. Visitors connected with the story, the ingredients, and the heart behind every jar. This was the moment Chlorice went from an idea to a brand people believed in.
